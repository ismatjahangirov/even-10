Iceland is a unique island nation known as the "Land of Fire and Ice" due to its volcanoes and glaciers. The country is famous for its breathtaking natural wonders like the Northern Lights and geothermal hot springs. Its capital, Reykjavik, is a vibrant city that runs almost entirely on renewable energy. Icelanders take great pride in their ancient Viking heritage and preserved language. It remains one of the safest and most peaceful countries in the world.
